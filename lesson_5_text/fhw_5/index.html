Syrnyky


Today I am going to teach you how to make syrnyky


How to make syrnyky

Ingredients:

  • 250 g cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2-3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla sugar (optional)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3-4 tablespoons flour (plus extra for dusting)
  • Oil or butter for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the cottage cheese, egg, sugar, vanilla sugar, and salt until smooth.
  2. Add the flour and mix gently to make a soft dough.
  3. Shape the dough into small round pancakes, lightly dusting them with flour.
  4. Heat oil or butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Fry the syrnyky for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  6. Place on paper towels to remove excess oil (if it's needed).
  7. Serve warm with sour cream, honey, jam, or berries.
