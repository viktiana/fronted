Feel every note of real coffee

Every drink is designed to feel warm, smooth, and quietly luxurious.

Soft Coffee-Tea is a modern café brand that blends specialty coffee, artisan tea, and handcrafted desserts into one cozy experience. We believe drinks should do more than wake you up - they should ground you. Our menu balances comfort and creativity, mixing classic flavors with soft, unexpected twists.

Our products

Velvet Cloud Latte

Moonleaf Matcha Tea
